What is your favorite thing about the Interactive Planner?

John.

I love that it is our own creation, something we will know from the inside out. There is a very appealing degree of intimacy involved with creating your own software, it seems to me.

Kylon.

Speaking from hindsight, the Interactive Planner was a great choice for a project one. The reason I say this is because of the mental challenge posed, at least for me. When the group got together and began brainstorming details about the interactive planner I was intimidated. Databases, Tkinter, and GUI's were foreign concepts to me, at least until group members began pointing the way and sharing more information about the project. Now, I have created and managed my own local database that is a small piece to a much bigger machine. I found that very cool!
